A Note on Reading Mathematics

People often talk about reading mathematics as if it were like reading a novel. It really is not.

A more realistic model: you are trying to compress a long argument into a small, robust mental structure. That requires:

  1. Skimming for global structure
  2. Filling in local details where your intuition fails
  3. Trying to reproduce the argument from memory

The posts on this site are written with that workflow in mind: they should be easy to skim, but also withstand careful reading.
