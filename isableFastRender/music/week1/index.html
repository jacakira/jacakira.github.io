Listening Log: Week 1

January 12, 2025

Some things I listened to this week:

  • An annoyingly catchy pop album
  • A dense modern jazz record that I only half-understand
  • A string quartet where the harmonic language felt weirdly “probabilistic”

One recurring thought: a lot of music feels like it is exploring a space of transformations rather than a fixed melody. That is not far from how I think about symmetry groups in mathematics.
